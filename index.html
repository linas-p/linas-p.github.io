About

My research is taking place under the supervision of Drs Romas Baronas at (MIF VU) on non-linear reaction-diffusion systems.

I was previously graduated in Mathematical Statistics at the Vilnius university (VU) (Lithuania) in 2015. This Master degree programme was delivered by the Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics.
